Hyderabad is a city in India. It is in the Telangana state of India. It is spread over an area of 650 square kilometers. Hyderabad was established in 1591 by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah. It is the joint capital of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. The official languages of Hyderabad are Urdu and Telugu. Some popular dishes in Hyderabad are Nihari, Chakna, Bhaghara Baingan, Qubani ka Meetha, and Kaddu ki kheer. Some famous places in Hyderabad are Charminar, Ramoji film city, Salar Jung Museum, and Golconda fort. It is also known as the ‘City of Pearls’. Hyderabad has a mixed cultural and religious background
